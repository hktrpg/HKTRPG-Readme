# Daily Entertainment

* `每日笑話` (Daily Joke) — Shows a joke
* `每日動漫` (Daily Anime Quote) — Shows an anime quote
* `每日一言` (Daily Quote) — Shows a quote
* `每日廢話` (Daily Nonsense) — Shows a random line
* `每日毒湯` (Daily Toxic Soup) — Shows a cynical “chicken soup” line
* `每日情話` (Daily Love Line) — Shows a romantic line
* `每日急口令` (Daily Tongue Twister) — Shows a tongue twister
* `每日大事` (Daily History) — Shows major events in history today (from Wikipedia)
